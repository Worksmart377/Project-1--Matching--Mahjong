Mahjong Solitaire

Game Instructions:

Your goal is to find a match for each tile and click them both to remove them from the board. Once you get to the last two tiles on the board YOU WON!
Rules: 1. You can only click a tile if it is exposed on the left or right side.
2. If you get stuck and don't see anymore matches then you can click "SHUFFLE" in order to randomize the tiles.
3. If you select your first tile and change your mind, you will have to reclick that tile to unselect it.
4. Enjoy your game!
